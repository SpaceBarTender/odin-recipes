Crab Dip

I never thought crab dip would be AMAZING until I met my wife. Dip some toasted french sliced bread into this for a tasty treat!

Here are the ingredients (serves 8):

  • 8 oz. lump crab meat (can or fresh)
  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp. mayo
  • 1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. mustard powder
  • 1 tbsp. milk
  • 1/4-1/2 cup cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 F.
  2. While preheating, mix everything but the crab
  3. Fold in the crab into the bowl
  4. Put into backing dish and add extra cheese on top
  5. Bake for 25 minutes
  6. Serve with baguette toasted, crackers, or pretzels
  7. Add salt and pepper to your liking
  8. Enjoy!
