Buffalo Chicken Dip

Delicious! This simple reicpe is great for football games, casual days, or even for a dinner appetizer.

Here are the ingredients (serves 8):

  • 8 oz. lump crab meat (can or fresh)
  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp. mayo
  • 1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. mustard powder
  • 1 tbsp. milk
  • 1/4-1/2 cup cheddar cheese
