Buffalo Chicken Dip

Delicious! This simple reicpe is great for football games, casual days, or even for a dinner appetizer.

Here are the ingredients (serves 8):

  • 1 lb. chicken breast cooked and shredded or 2-4 cans of chicken
  • 8 oz. cream cheese softened
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce of your liking (I like Sweet Baby Ray's)
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese (I like colby jack or cheddar, for extra kick choose a mix of pepper chack and cheddar

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 F
  2. Mix all ingredients (but just 1 cup of cheese) in a bowl
  3. Put mix into a backing dish and bake for 10 minutes
  4. Add last 1/2 cup of cheese on top
  5. Bake for another 5-10 minutes or until cheese is melted
  6. Slice french bread and toast in oven
  7. Use bread to dip with and enjoy!
